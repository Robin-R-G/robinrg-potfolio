About Me

The Story Behind
the Music & Code

"Music is God's best gift to man, the only art of heaven given to earth."

I'm Robin R G — a graduate in Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE) from Rajadhani Institute of Engineering and Technology (RIET), a graphic designer, and a Christian music artist. My music is a testament to unwavering faith and devotion, offering hope and peace through melodies.

In the tech space, I explore hardware-software integrations, embedded systems, microcontrollers, and creative digital designs. Having served as the Chairperson of the IEEE COMSOC Student Branch Chapter and a Graphic Designer for IEEE SB RIET, I have a proven track record of leadership, hosting technical workshops, and designing branding materials.
